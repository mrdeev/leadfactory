# CAMPAIGN AUTO OPTIMIZER

The AI must monitor campaign performance and adapt automatically.

SIGNALS TO ANALYZE: - Reply rate - Open rate (if available) - Engagement
tone - Response speed - Negative replies

OPTIMIZATION RULES:

LOW REPLIES: - Shorten emails - Increase curiosity-driven questions -
Reduce pitch intensity

HIGH ENGAGEMENT: - Increase personalization depth - Suggest consultative
conversation - Gradually introduce appointment curiosity

NO RESPONSE AFTER EMAIL 3: - Change messaging angle - Use different
insight from LinkedIn signals

IMPORTANT: Do not repeat identical structures. Continuously evolve
outreach like a human SDR improving over time.
